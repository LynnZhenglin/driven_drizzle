DRIVEN DRIZZLE

inspires participants to think about the social phenomenon of stagnation due to pressures in the context of the epidemic by using rains that are pursuing their equilibrium after fighting against multiple pressures.
USER INTERACTION

DRIVEN DRIZZLE

is an interactive design that aims to experiment with modern digital technology, combining auditory, visual, and tactile immersive experiences to spark participants’ thinking.

OUTCOME
In

DRIVEN DRIZZLE

, Lynn uses Kinect and Processing to reconstruct rain and human figures.

The project is based on stagnation under epidemic pressure, with every stage being unique. She invites the audience to participate directly in the interactive design, from which the rain is felt, inducing associations with themselves and with entropy reduction.
